The Shawshank Redemption

Actors | Quotes

Summary

The Shawshank Redemption is a tale of hope, friendship, and resilience. It follows Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongly imprisoned for the murder of his wife and her lover. Through his friendship with Red, another inmate, Andy learns to navigate the brutal prison system and eventually orchestrates a daring escape, leaving behind a legacy of hope.

Actors

Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne and Morgan Freeman as Ellis "Red" Redding deliver outstanding performances that bring depth and humanity to the story.

Quotes
